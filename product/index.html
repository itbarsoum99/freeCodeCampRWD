Replenishing Rainforests


Our mission

The mission of Pacific is to plant trees, restore wildlife, and protect rainforests through grassroots activism, fundraising, and petitioning - and to prevent the destruction of natural habitats in rainforests around the globe for industrial, agricultural, or other use.

Our impact

Pacific has planted 3,963,424 trees, has fueled the resurgence of 37 species, and has supported the successful passage of 226 pieces of environmental legislation in congresses and parliaments around the world.
